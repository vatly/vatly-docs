Welcome to Vatly

Vatly is the Merchant of Record for European SaaS companies, enabling you to sell worldwide through a single API.

Vatly takes care of the hard parts of selling software globally -- tax compliance, billing, invoicing -- so you can focus on building your product. Handle subscriptions, one-off payments, and global tax compliance through a single API.

Hosted Checkouts

Create hosted checkout sessions with one API call. Support for one-off products and subscription plans with automatic tax calculation.

Subscriptions

Full subscription lifecycle management. Handle upgrades, downgrades, proration, trials, and cancellations.

Orders & Invoices

Every payment generates an order with automatic tax calculation and invoicing. Vatly handles EU VAT, US sales tax, and reporting as your Merchant of Record.

Webhooks

Real-time event notifications for checkouts, orders, subscriptions, refunds, and chargebacks.

Get started in minutes

  1. Create a new account at vatly.com.
  2. Grab your API keys from Settings > API.
  3. Connect your Mollie account.
  4. Set up your products.
  5. Integrate Vatly using one of our packages.
